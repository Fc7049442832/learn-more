MP Police Constable 2023

The MP Police Constable syllabus for 2023 covers a wide range of subjects to assess candidates' knowledge and skills.
It includes topics such as:

  • General Knowledge : This section tests candidates' knowledge of current affairs, history, geography, Indian culture, and more.

  • Reasoning : This section tests candidates' ability to think logically and solve problems.

  • Science : This section tests candidates' knowledge of basic science concepts, such as physics, chemistry, and biology.

  • Simple Arithmetic : This section tests candidates' ability to perform basic arithmetic operations, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

  • Intellectual Ability & Mental Aptitude : This section tests candidates' ability to think critically and solve problems.
